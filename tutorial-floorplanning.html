What is Floorplanning?

Floorplanning is the process of arranging different blocks of the chip before placement and routing. The goal is to optimize performance, power, and area (PPA).

Key Steps

  • Define chip dimensions and aspect ratio.
  • Plan power grid (VDD & VSS straps).
  • Place macros and memory blocks.
  • Reserve routing channels and keep-out zones.

Example TCL Snippet

set_die_area {0 0 2000 2000}
set_core_area {50 50 1950 1950}
add_power_stripes -nets {VDD VSS} -layer metal5 -width 2
